Fun Facts

Bite-sized trivia pulled from the archives.

Doraemon's ears were originally round — a mouse chewed them off, which is why he's afraid of mice.

He was blue-and-white originally, but turned fully blue after crying, which washed off his yellow paint.

His favorite food is dorayaki, a red-bean-filled pancake sandwich — he'll do almost anything for one.

The 4D pocket is said to store well over a thousand different gadgets across the full franchise.

Doraemon's "birthday" (manufacture date) is listed as September 3, 2112.

The series has been translated and broadcast in dozens of countries and languages worldwide.
